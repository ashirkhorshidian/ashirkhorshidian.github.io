Northwood M70-D

A great mahogany dreadnought with a red spruce top.

The Northwood M70-D is a 14-fret square-shoulder dreadnought. Handmade by John McQuarrie in Canada.

  • Adirondack spruce top
  • Mahogany back and sides
  • 25 1/2" scale length
  • 1 3/4" nut width
  • Ebony fingerboard, bridge, and binding for the body
  • Herringbone trim purfling and rosette
  • Bone nut, saddle, and bridge pins

It has excellent volume, clarity, punch, and balance. Perfect for flatpicking and bluegrass.
